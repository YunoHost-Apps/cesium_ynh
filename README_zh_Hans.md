## 概况

[Cesium](https://cesium.app) is an [Unhosted webapp](https://unhosted.org) client for any [Duniter](https://duniter.org) crypto-currency.
It allows you to manage your wallet, certify your friends, and more!


**分发版本：** 1.7.13~ynh2
